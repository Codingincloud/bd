Health Tips for Donors

  • Eat iron-rich foods like spinach, red meat, and beans
  • Drink plenty of water before and after donation
  • Regular exercise helps maintain healthy blood levels
  • Get adequate sleep (7-8 hours) before donation
  • Avoid alcohol 24 hours before donation
