How to Measure Your Health Metrics

  • Weight: Use a digital scale, weigh yourself at the same time each day
  • Blood Pressure: Use a home blood pressure monitor or visit a pharmacy
  • Heart Rate: Place two fingers on your wrist, count beats for 15 seconds, multiply by 4
  • Best Time: Measure in the morning before eating or exercising
