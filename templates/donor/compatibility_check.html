Blood Type Facts

Universal Donor

O- blood type can donate to all blood types, making O- donors extremely valuable in emergencies.

Universal Receiver

AB+ blood type can receive from all blood types, but can only donate to other AB+ recipients.

Most Common

O+ is the most common blood type, found in about 38% of the population.

Rarest

AB- is one of the rarest blood types, found in less than 1% of the population.

Important Information

  • Safe Donation: Always donate only to compatible blood types
  • Medical Supervision: All donations must be done under medical supervision
  • Donation Frequency: Wait at least 3 months between donations
  • Health Check: Regular health screening is required for all donors
