Understanding Blood Compatibility

Blood compatibility is crucial for safe transfusions. Here's what you need to know:

  • Universal Donor: O- blood can be given to anyone
  • Universal Recipient: AB+ can receive from any blood type
  • Rh Factor: Rh+ can receive Rh+ or Rh-, but Rh- can only receive Rh-
  • Antigens: A blood has A antigens, B blood has B antigens, AB has both, O has neither

Your blood type ({{ donor.blood_group|default:"A+" }}) is compatible with recipients who can safely receive your blood type. Always consult with medical professionals for any transfusion decisions.
