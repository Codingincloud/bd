Algorithm

1. Start: Initialize BDIMS and establish database connection.

2. Display Home Page: Show login and registration options.

3. User Authentication:
   a. Register: Enter credentials and role (Donor/Admin), provide required details 
      (blood group for donor, hospital info for admin), validate and create account.
   b. Login: Enter credentials and role, verify against database, create session 
      and redirect to dashboard.

4. Donor Dashboard Operations:
   a. View profile, total donations, eligibility status, and health metrics.
   b. Check donation eligibility based on 56-day rule.
   c. Schedule donation by selecting hospital, date, and time.
   d. View donation history and request statuses.
   e. Update health metrics (weight, blood pressure, heart rate).
   f. View and respond to emergency blood requests.
   g. Update location using GPS, predefined cities, or manual entry.
   h. View blood inventory across all hospitals.
   i. Update personal information and emergency contacts.
   j. View notifications and alerts.

5. Admin Dashboard Operations:
   a. View system analytics and statistics.
   b. Manage donation requests (approve, reject, or mark complete).
   c. Manage blood inventory (add, remove, or set units).
   d. Create and manage emergency blood requests.
   e. Track and search donors with filters.
   f. Manage hospital details and settings.
   g. Search donors by location and blood group.
   h. Generate and export reports.

6. Blood Compatibility: O- donates to all, O+ to O+/A+/B+/AB+, A- to 
   A-/A+/AB-/AB+, A+ to A+/AB+, B- to B-/B+/AB-/AB+, B+ to B+/AB+, AB- to 
   AB-/AB+, AB+ to AB+ only.

7. Database Operations: Validate inputs, execute queries, use transactions, 
   update timestamps, create notifications, maintain integrity.

8. Logout: Clear session and redirect to login.

9. End: Return to step 2.
